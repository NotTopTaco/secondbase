# SecondBase

**A real-time MLB analytics companion — built by a fan, for fans.**

SecondBase is a passion project born from a simple idea: what if you could have a baseball analyst sitting next to you while you watch the game? Not the kind on TV talking over the broadcast — your own personal second screen that quietly surfaces the stats, matchups, and insights that make every at-bat more interesting.

This isn't a startup. There's no business plan. It's just a love letter to baseball and the endless depth hiding behind every pitch.

## What It Does

When you open SecondBase during a game, you get a dashboard of live analytics panels — each one answering a different question you might have as a fan:

- **Strike Zone** — See every pitch plotted in real time, color-coded by type
- **Hot Zones** — Where does this batter crush the ball? Where does he struggle?
- **Pitcher Tendencies** — What does this pitcher like to throw, and when?
- **Batter vs. Pitch Type** — How does the hitter perform against fastballs vs. sliders vs. curveballs?
- **Spray Chart** — Where does this batter tend to hit the ball on the field?
- **Head-to-Head** — The full history between the current batter and pitcher
- **Next Pitch Probability** — What's the pitcher likely to throw next, based on the count and situation?
- **Win Probability** — A live chart showing each team's chances of winning as the game unfolds
- **Pitcher Fatigue** — Is the pitcher losing velocity? Might a pitching change be coming?
- **Time Through the Order** — How does this pitcher perform the second and third time facing the lineup?
- **Umpire Scouting** — How does today's home plate umpire call the strike zone? Does he favor inside or outside pitches?
- **Pitch Movement** — How much does each pitch type break horizontally and vertically?
- **Bullpen Status** — Who's available in the bullpen and how fresh are they?
- **Batter by Count** — How dangerous is this hitter when ahead vs. behind in the count?
- **Streak Indicator** — Is the batter or pitcher on a hot or cold streak?
- **Defensive Positioning** — Where is the defense shifting for this batter?
- **Pitch Tunneling** — How well does the pitcher disguise different pitches to look the same out of the hand?

You can drag and drop the panels to arrange them however you like, and the app updates automatically as the game progresses.

## The Homepage

When you first log in, you'll see today's MLB schedule — every game happening that day. Games involving your favorite teams are highlighted at the top. If you've picked favorite players, you'll also see a "Your Players Today" card showing how they're doing across all of today's games, updating live.

Tap any game card to jump into the full companion view with all the analytics panels.

## Personalizing Your Experience

From your profile page, you can:

- **Pick your favorite teams** — Their games get surfaced first, and you'll see them highlighted in the sidebar while watching other games
- **Pick your favorite players** — Track how they're doing across all of today's games from the homepage

## While Watching a Game

Once you're in a game, a sidebar shows you what's happening across the rest of the league — your teams' games, any games you've pinned, and other live action. It's easy to keep one eye on the scores around the league while diving deep into the game you're watching.

## Why "SecondBase"?

It's your second screen. For baseball. That's it. (Okay, it's also a baseball pun. Every good project needs at least one.)
